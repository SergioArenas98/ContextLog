ContextLog Privacy Policy Effective date: April 11, 2026 ContextLog is an Android app for archaeological field recording. Information we collect ContextLog allows users to create and manage archaeological records, including features, contexts, finds, samples, photos, drawings, and related notes. The information entered into the app is stored locally on the user's device. Photos and media ContextLog may request access to the device camera and photos so users can take or select reference images for archaeological records. These images are stored locally on the device for app functionality. Data sharing ContextLog does not sell user data. ContextLog does not share personal data with third parties for advertising or marketing. Data storage Data created in the app is stored locally on the device.
